## Ordinary Differential Equations (ODE) Summary

### GBM Cells (IDH1-Mutant)
- **Metabolites Tracked:**
  - Glucose, Lactate
  - Î±-Ketoglutarate (aKG)
  - 2-Hydroxyglutarate (2-HG)
  - NAD+/NADH
  - SIRT1 Activity
- **Key Dynamics:**
  - IDH1 mutation diverts aKG to 2-HG.
  - Resveratrol increases SIRT1 via NAD+ boost.
  - 2-HG suppresses antigen expression via TET inhibition.

### CAR-Macrophages
- **Tracked Variables:**
  - Energy Pool (ATP)
  - NAD+/NADH
  - SIRT1 Activity
- **Interactions:**
  - Resveratrol restores NAD+ and boosts energy.
  - Energy depletion governs exhaustion state transitions.
